About me

I am passionate about the web.

After taking various “career quizzes” online, I happened upon the website for Skillcrush - an online tech education company. I’ve always been interested in coding, but never thought I’d be able to do it. To my surprise, Skillcrush seemed to be a lot more inclusive and supportive than any other online coding course I’d come across. I signed up immediately and, as evidenced by this hand-coded website, it’s been a success so far. I haven’t been this excited since Avatar: the Last Airbender got a sequel.

My favorite thing about web development is that it’s creative and technical; it suits me well. I’m still learning, but if you’d like to talk about a future project, web development in general, or even Avatar: the Last Airbender, feel free to contact me. I look forward to hearing from you.
